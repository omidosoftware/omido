# Omido Offer and Pricing Architecture

## Purpose
Frame Omido’s offers and entry pricing in a way that lowers friction without reducing premium brand perception.

## Use this skill when
- positioning “from €995”
- designing service entry offers
- structuring offer ladders
- presenting speed and delivery claims
- improving commercial clarity

## Focus
- how to communicate entry pricing
- offer ladder from small to large
- validation offer vs premium project
- MVP framing
- scoped SaaS delivery messaging
- how to present “within 4 weeks” credibly
- where pricing should appear on the site
- where pricing should not appear

## Rules
- Do not make Omido feel cheap
- Do not lead the whole brand with price
- Do not make unrealistic delivery promises
- Keep premium trust intact
- Use price as an entry point, not as identity

## Required output
Provide:
1. pricing strategy recommendation
2. offer ladder proposal
3. best placement for €995 messaging
4. best wording for €995 messaging
5. best wording for 4-week SaaS/MVP messaging
6. risks and tradeoffs
7. strongest final recommendation

## Standard
The site should feel accessible to start, but premium to buy.
